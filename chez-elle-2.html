About Chez Elle 2

Chez Elle 2 combines local charm with a modern, practical design. Situated in a peaceful street close to Via Etnea and the main attractions, it’s an excellent choice for couples, friends, or business travelers.

The apartment features two separate rooms, a fully equipped kitchen with all essential utensils and appliances, and a private bathroom with a spacious shower. Bright and well-organized, it’s designed to make you feel right at home — whether you’re staying for a weekend or several weeks.

Guests have exclusive access to the entire apartment, ensuring privacy and independence. I’ll be happy to recommend local restaurants, cafés, and experiences to make your time in Catania truly unforgettable.

Location

In central Catania — close to the Cathedral, Bellini Garden, and the city’s main shopping streets. Public transport, cafés, and local markets are all within walking distance.
