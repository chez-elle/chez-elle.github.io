About Dimoretta

Welcome to a penthouse designed for memorable stays — generous interiors, a truly enormous panoramic terrace, and curated finishes that combine modern comfort with a Sicilian soul.

The huge living room is ideal for gathering with family or friends, while the fully equipped kitchen makes cooking effortless — from espresso mornings to sunset dinners outdoors. With 3 bedrooms and 3 bathrooms, everyone gets space and privacy.

Step outside to the terrace and enjoy amazing views of Mt. Etna and the sea. Whether you’re here to relax or work remotely, you’ll appreciate fast Wi-Fi, A/C, and smooth self check-in.

Location

Central Catania — steps from Via Etnea, the Cathedral, and Bellini Garden. Cafés, markets, and public transport nearby.
