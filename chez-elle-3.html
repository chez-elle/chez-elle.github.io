About Chez Elle 3

Designed for effortless stays, this studio blends Sicilian warmth with modern comfort. You’re on a quiet street near Via Etnea, the Duomo, and local markets — an ideal base for sightseeing, work, or a weekend escape.

Inside you’ll find a comfy double bed, a compact but well-equipped kitchen corner (essentials, utensils, small appliances), and a clean bathroom with a quality shower. The space is bright, efficient, and easy to love.

You’ll have the studio all to yourself for full privacy and independence. I’m happy to share local tips on restaurants, cafés, and experiences to help you enjoy Catania like a local.

Location

Central Catania — walk to the Cathedral, Bellini Garden, and the city’s main shopping streets. Public transport, cafés, and local markets are close by.
